# kWh to RM Calculation Guide

## Formula
Cost (RM) = Total kWh consumed × Tariff rate (RM/kWh)

### Example (Malaysia - TNB)
- Tariff: RM 0.57/kWh
- Consumption: 100 kWh
- Calculation: 100 × 0.57 = RM 57

### Notes
- Tariffs vary by region and time-of-use
- Include service charges if required
- Calculation accuracy matches TNB bill formula closely
