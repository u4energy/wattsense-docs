# Competitor Positioning - Lotus West

## Overview

Walaupun Lotus West dan Wattsense berada dalam industri yang sama, kedua-duanya menyasarkan segmen pasaran yang berbeza.

---

## Lotus West

### Positioning

Energy Engineering & Consultancy Company

### Primary Customers

* Factories
* Commercial Buildings
* Large Facilities
* Industrial Clients

### Business Model

* Consultancy
* EPC Projects
* Engineering Services

### Characteristics

* High project value
* Long sales cycle
* Custom implementation
* Consultancy intensive

---

## Wattsense

### Positioning

Energy Intelligence & Cost Optimisation Platform

### Primary Customers

* SMEs
* Retail Stores
* Homestays
* Small Offices
* Residential Users

### Business Model

* SaaS Subscription
* Product-Led Growth
* Recurring Revenue

### Characteristics

* Affordable
* Fast deployment
* Plug-and-play
* Scalable platform

---

## Strategic Difference

Lotus West sells:

Energy Projects

Wattsense sells:

Energy Intelligence

---

## Key Positioning Statement

Wattsense does not sell IoT devices.

Wattsense helps customers reduce electricity costs, eliminate energy waste and improve operational efficiency through intelligent monitoring, automation and actionable insights.
